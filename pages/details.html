Sharm El Sheikh International Airport is 15 minutes from Amar Sina Hotel. Naama Bay promenade is 5 miles away, where there are a variety of restaurants and shops. An airport shuttle is available upon request.

The trip’s details and program

In Ras Umm Elsid in Sharm El Sheikh, Amar Sina Hotel features panoramic views of Sinai Mountains. Featuring a private beach, the hotel has an outdoor swimming pool, a spa and a diving center. The air-conditioned rooms feature a window overlooking the pool or the desert. Each has a small sitting area with satellite TV and a minibar. The bathroom has a shower and a bathtub.
